NEW SAC & OAT

SAC is the hub for most of the major extra-curricular activities. It has rooms for all the clubs of Student's Gymkhana. There are two SAC buildings, the older building(Old-SAC) and the other (New-SAC), which was recently built. New-SAC has an open-air theatre (OAT) with a capacity of over 1400 people. The SAC is maintained and managed by the Students' Gymkhana at IIT Kanpur.

It is used in almost every inter-hall competition like Galaxy, Takneek etc and for many shows in the institute. We also have a projector and a great sound system. Our Film and Media Club arranges the screening of various movies in a regular time period.
Various Functions are organized here every year. Some are:
1. Fresher's Night is organized every year for 1st year students.
2. Various Performances of Udghosh (Annual Sports Festival of IIT Kanpur) are organized.
3. All the events of Takneek, Galaxy, Techkriti (Inter-Hall Technical Competition) are held here.
There is also a food court where we have food available 22.5 hours of the day. It has shops vending s nacks, Rolls, Dosas etc. We can have food there till 4:00 am.
